3. EQUIPMENT RESPONSIBILITY: I am financially responsible for any loss, theft, or damage to equipment beyond normal wear and tear. Equipment must be returned in the condition received.

4. SAFETY COMPLIANCE: I agree to wear provided life jackets/PFDs when required and follow all instructions given by APR team members at all times.
